About the Task

Understanding the real-world context

In real businesses, companies collect massive amounts of retail sales data from stores and online platforms. Understanding this data helps businesses identify trends, customer demand, seasonal patterns, and top-performing products.

However, raw sales data is often messy and difficult to interpret.

In this task, you will perform Retail Sales Data Analysis to uncover meaningful insights from real-world datasets. You will learn how to clean data, analyze sales patterns, and build visual dashboards that help businesses make smarter decisions.

This is the kind of work real data analysts and business intelligence teams perform in companies.

Objective

Your mission for this task

Your objective is to analyze retail sales datasets and discover business insights such as:

Sales trends over time
Product performance
Category-level sales comparison
Regional sales performance
Seasonal demand patterns

You must present your findings using clear visualizations and dashboards.
