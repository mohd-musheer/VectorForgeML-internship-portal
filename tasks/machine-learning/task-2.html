About the Task

Understanding the real-world context

Customer retention is one of the most important challenges for modern businesses.

Companies such as telecom providers, banks, and e-commerce platforms lose customers every year due to competition, pricing, or poor customer experience.

Machine Learning allows businesses to analyze historical customer behavior and predict which customers are likely to leave. These predictions help companies take proactive actions to retain valuable customers.

In this task, you will build a Customer Churn Prediction model using machine learning techniques. You will analyze customer behavior data and train a classification model to predict whether a customer is likely to churn.

This type of analysis is widely used by data science and machine learning teams in real companies.

Objective

Your mission for this task

Your objective is to build a machine learning classification model that predicts whether a customer will churn. You should analyze customer features and identify patterns that influence churn behavior.

Examples of insights you may explore:

Customer attributes influencing churn
Churn probability predictions
Feature importance analysis
Model accuracy and performance evaluation

You should also visualize your predictions and explain the results clearly.
